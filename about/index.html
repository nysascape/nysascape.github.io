Hi! Welcome to my about page.

Termius is my #1 tool of choice. It’s an amazing SSH client and allows me to be always connected to my VPS - for which I use Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure. I’m looking to get a dedicated 24h VPS soon, such as Vultr. For my IDE, I have a subscription to the JetBrains suite for Linux, and I, of course, use Microsoft VS 2019 for Windows. VS Code is my portable editor of choice and I could not exist without the Office 365 suite.

In my personal life, I like to skate, surf, take photos, and love music. I spend a lot of time with my amazing girlfriend and try to balance my time between programming, studies and life - it’s really important to not burn yourself out.

If you see anyone with the username nysadev, or nysascape, you can be mostly sure it’s me.
